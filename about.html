Hey you!

I'm Gabija Sura, a Stockholm based designer with a passion for motion and UX design. I hold a bachelor's degree in Media Technology from Södertörn University, where I gained skills in UX, interaction design, graphic design, and coding. The most valuable lesson I learned during those three years? Great design starts with thorough research and a deep understanding of the problem at hand.

Currently, I'm diving deeper into the world of motion design through the Motion Creative program at Hyper Island, where I'm exploring new ways to bring ideas to life through movement and storytelling.

When I'm not designing, you'll find me expressing my creativity through arts and crafts. I'm especially passionate about crochet — making everything from accessories and clothes to home decor. I also love being outdoors, hiking, and exploring new places.
