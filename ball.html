Pass the Ball

This animation is part of a collaborative project created by my class at Hyper Island. The only rules were that there had to be a ball-like shape crossing the screen, and the ball could be anything we wanted. This video combines all our unique creations, showcasing our creativity, individuality, and teamwork in one piece.
