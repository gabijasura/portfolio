Yennenga

This animation was created for Yenenga Progress, a non-profit organization dedicated to sustainable development in Burkina Faso. The goal was to produce an engaging and heartfelt animation to encourage monthly donations, showing the tangible impact that consistent support has on education, nutrition, and community empowerment.

Through a mix of storytelling and motion design, we aimed to highlight that Yenenga Progress provides much more than aid — they create long-term change.

I was the Producer for this project, but even though we had defined roles, our team worked collaboratively throughout the process. I managed workflows, timelines, and communication while also integrating feedback and balancing workloads. Since our team dynamic was so hands-on, I even got to animate some elements in a few scenes, making this an even more rewarding experience.

Final Outcome

The animation was successfully launched at an event and shared by Yenenga Progress, helping them amplify their message and attract new monthly donors. The project was a rewarding experience that reinforced my passion for smooth creative workflows, team coordination, and impactful storytelling through motion design.

The final video was published on Yenenga Progress's official website as part of their communication to attract new monthly donors.
