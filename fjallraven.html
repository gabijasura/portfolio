Fjällräven

During my internship at Bsmart, I worked on an internal presentation for Fjällräven showcasing their upcoming Spring/Summer 2026 collection. The highlight of the project was bringing the Kånken Graphics backpack to life — a series featuring two distinct animal-themed designs.

I created frame-by-frame animations where the animals on each backpack — a seal and a bird — literally came to life, transforming static product images into playful, animated moments.
